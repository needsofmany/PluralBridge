Contact

Contact PluralBridge

PluralBridge is an independent project focused on helping Systems preserve, understand, and eventually move their own Simply Plural data.

General contact

For general questions about PluralBridge, the public website, project direction, or conference follow-up, email info@thepluralbridge.org.

Support / export help

For help with the public Simply Plural export guidance, email support@thepluralbridge.org.

Please describe where you are stuck in the export process. Keep private System details out of the message.

Conference booth note

If you met PluralBridge through the Power to the Plurals Conference booth, these project-level addresses are the safest way to follow up after the booth.

Privacy reminder

Do not send Simply Plural tokens, passwords, export files, private System data, or screenshots that contain private information.

PluralBridge is being built around privacy and data ownership. Public threads, email, and direct messages are not safe places to share sensitive System data.

Not emergency support

PluralBridge is not emergency support. Do not use PluralBridge contact addresses for crisis, safety, medical, legal, or emergency requests.

If someone is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services or a crisis support service in your area.
