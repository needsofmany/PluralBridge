Task 22 design: Audit seam
2026-06-22 12:12 AM PT

Boundary name: Chapter 2 Audit Seam

The audit seam is the future evidence boundary for the request spine.

Audit seam placement:
1. After current account resolution.
2. After membership resolution.
3. After current system resolution.
4. After authorization decision.
5. Before or around future evidence-worthy data access or mutation points.

Future audit records may reference:
- account identity
- system identity
- membership identity
- authorization decision
- operation name
- trace or correlation identity

Task 22 names the seam only.
Task 22 does not create audit storage.
Task 22 does not add audit writes.
Task 22 does not add audit triggers.
Task 22 does not change runtime behavior.
